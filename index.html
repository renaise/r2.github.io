Renaise is a brand + product designer working across culture + venture in consumer media, creative tools, and deep tech. Founder of Codex Foundry. Creative director of Artifice NYC. Design partner of Studio Artifice. Renaise's focus is on open curatorial research and showcasing artifice: the artifacts of science fiction. Her practice is focused on web-to-world applications via science + fiction (proxy for prototyping).
